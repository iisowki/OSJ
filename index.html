Jason Thanh Nguyen (born May 9, 2004), better known online as JasonTheWeen (sometimes styled Jasontheween or JasonTheWeenie), is a Vietnamese-American content creator, streamer, musician, and rising internet personality.

He started streaming simply because gaming with friends was already hilarious — so he figured, why not bring more people into the chaos? Since then, he’s grown a loyal community that shows up not just for the games, but for the personality, the jokes, and the goofy “Ween Lore” that evolves every stream.
